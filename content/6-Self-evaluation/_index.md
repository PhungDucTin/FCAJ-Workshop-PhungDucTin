### Areas for Improvement

*   **Troubleshooting mindset:** Need to develop the ability to analyze system logs (CloudWatch, WAF logs) in a more multidimensional and systematic way, thereby shortening the time to find the root cause of complex technical issues.
*   **Technical communication skills:** Improve communication abilities, learning how to present system architectures or security risks more coherently, concisely, and comprehensibly to both technical and non-technical teams.
*   **Compliance and optimal operations:** Continue to train personal discipline in adhering to AWS security best practices (such as Least Privilege) right from the initialization step, rather than just adjusting when errors occur.
